About & Philosophy

About Us

First Principles Advisory Group is a boutique management consultancy focused exclusively on the healthcare industry. We were founded in 2010 and are located in the San Francisco Bay Area.

We set ourselves apart from typical life sciences consultancies in several ways: (1) we focus on both strategy and the operational implementation of our work; (2) we provide seasoned partners and project leaders as dedicated points of contact on all our projects; (3) we exhibit a breadth and depth of in-house industry and consulting experiences.

We have extensive, long term relationships with large, Fortune 500 public companies, mid-size firms, well-financed early stage companies, and venture capital firms.

Our therapeutic areas of expertise cover all areas, including:

  • Oncology (e.g., solid tumors, heme/onc; immunotherapy including CAR-T and bispecifics, targeted combination strategies, including companion diagnostics (cDx))
  • Immunology (e.g., rheumatoid arthritis, psoriasis, asthma, IPF, atopic dermatitis; including cDx)
  • Orphan diseases
  • Pulmonary critical care
  • Infectious diseases (e.g., antivirals, antibacterials, HIV/AIDS, TB)
  • Cardiovascular
  • Central nervous system (CNS)
  • Gastrointestinal (e.g., Crohn’s, UC)
  • Renal (e.g., chronic kidney disease)
  • Ophthalmology (e.g,. wet AMD, GA)
  • Pain
  • Wound care

Our Philosophy

  • We form business hypotheses, then test them with data-driven analyses.
  • We work collaboratively, flexibly, and iteratively -- adapting our working style to your needs, whether hands-off or seeking us to extend your in-house team.
  • We cultivate long-term relationships built on honesty and trust.
  • For market research: we integrate ourselves to understand your problems and how we can solve them; we facilitate all our fieldwork ourselves.

Services

  • Market research (e.g., qualitative or quantitative, and in many various forms such as conjoint, maxdiff, focus groups, etc.)
  • R&D strategy (e.g., individual program or multi-program investment strategies; high-level clinical development strategy)
  • Portfolio management (e.g., portfolio strategy; data systems and decision making tools; organization and governance)
  • Brand strategy (e.g., new indication evaluation, launch planning, new market growth strategy)
  • Medical affairs strategy (e.g., Phase IV strategy, strategic planning)
  • Opportunity evaluation (e.g., thoughtful, granular revenue forecasting; discounted cash flow modeling; decision tree analysis; Monte Carlo analysis)
  • Corporate growth strategy (e.g., fund raising strategy, go-to-market strategy, build vs. buy strategy)
